About Us

Our Mission

At SMAAC STEM, we aim to connect people with opportunities in STEM (science, technology, engineering and mathematics), whether that is directing junior explorers to websites where they can explore their interests, linking explorers to opportunities in our community tab or providing parents with great ideas to foster an interest in STEM in their children.

Our goal is to:

  • Encourage students to improve their STEM skills
  • Motivate parents and students to explore interests in STEM
  • Connect students to opportunities to achieve their aspirations
  • Allow students to improve their analysis skills
  • Provide students with a space to foster their curiosity
